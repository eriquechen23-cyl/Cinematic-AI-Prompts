*   **幻象:** `Kodak Vision3 250D` + `Diffusion Filter`。偏暖，色彩豐富，柔光。
*   **色彩腳本:**
    *   Clip 01: Steel Blue & Grey.
    *   Clip 02: Deep Black & Orange.
    *   Clip 03: Gold & Red.
    *   Clip 04: Pure White & Gold.
    *   Clip 05: Pale Blue & White.

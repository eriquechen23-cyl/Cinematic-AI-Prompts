# 05_製作規格 (Production Specs)

###  美術指導 (Production Designer)
*   **核心色盤:**
    *   **City:** #001133 (Midnight Blue), #8800FF (Neon Purple), #00CCFF (Cyan).
    *   **Church:** #FFAA33 (Amber), #553300 (Dark Wood), #FFDD88 (Candle Glow).
*   **道具清單:**
    *   Takeout coffee cup (generic, white lid).
    *   Brown leather briefcase (worn texture).
    *   White candles (simple, wax texture).
    *   Nativity set (wooden, simple carving).

###  服裝設計 (Costume Designer)
*   **Chris:**
    *   Dark Grey Wool Coat (Texture: Felt/Wool).
    *   White Dress Shirt (slightly wrinkled).
    *   Dark Blue Tie (loosened).
*   **Little Girl:**
    *   Red knitted sweater with reindeer pattern.
    *   Red Santa hat with white pom-pom.

###  攝影指導 (DOP)
*   **Camera:** Arri Alexa Mini LF.
*   **Lens:** Cooke Anamorphic /i SF (Special Flare) - for beautiful oval bokeh and flares.
*   **Aspect Ratio:** 2.39:1.
*   **Lighting Strategy:**
    *   **Exterior:** High contrast, rim lighting from neon signs.
    *   **Interior:** Soft, motivated lighting from candles (practical lights).

###  特效指導 (VFX Supervisor)
*   **Particles:** Floating dust particles in church light shafts.
*   **Atmosphere:** Light fog/haze in the alleyway to catch the light.
*   **Glow:** Soft bloom/halation on candle flames.
